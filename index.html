About us

Mohit Kedia & Associates is a premier Chartered Accountancy Firm, focused on rendering services and adding value to its clients across the globe by providing highly efficient, technical and professional services. Founded by CA Mohit Kumar Kedia in the year 2021 with a vision to achieve maximum client satisfaction through the deployment of highly motivated, knowledgeable and problem-solving resources. The team comprises of dedicated professional possessing expertise across a range of business needs. Our success is directly related as well as inclined with the success of our clients. We deliver the services you need in a professional, fast, friendly and cost-efficient manner. Facilitating our clients overcome the challenges that the modern business has imposed is the core strategy that we brings in to grow, sustain and succeed organizational goals and challenges.
